## Summary

This study evaluated force perception induced by changes in an agent’s facial expression. The study examined how social visual stimuli, such as facial expressions, may influence haptic or pseudo-haptic perception.

The study contributes to the Measuring and HCI lines by experimentally arranging social cues and measuring their effects on perceived force. It also provides a foundation for later work on the relation between facial expressions and haptic perception.

## Research line

* **Measuring**: experimental measurement of perception induced by social visual cues
* **HCI**: agent expression, pseudo-haptic feedback, and interaction design
* **Haptics**: evaluation of force perception in response to facial expression changes
